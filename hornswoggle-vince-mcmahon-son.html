How Long Did Hornswoggle Know He Would Be McMahon's Son

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 27, 2024

It is one of the most controversial and disturbing storylines in the history of the WWE. On the Sept. 10, 2007 episode of Monday Night Raw, Hornswoggle was revealed to be the son of WWE chairman, Vince McMahon.

The whole storyline received backlash and heavy criticism from wrestling fans and pundits. Thankfully, the storyline only lasted a few short months - concluding with John Bradshaw Layfield announcing that Finlay was the real father of Hornswoggle.

Hornswoggle recently appeared on the Pancakes and Powerslams Show and went into full detail about how he found out that Vince McMahon would become his storyline father:

“Two hours. I thought it was a complete joke, and it was awesome to me because it was my hometown. Bruce Prichard came up to me and he said, ‘I need your phone.’ [I said], ‘for what?’ [He said], ‘I need your phone.’ [I responded] ‘Okay?’ He held my phone for the night so it wouldn’t get out, and told me.”

WWE had been building up towards a storyline that hinted at Mr. Kennedy being the father of Hornswoggle. But for unknown reasons, the creative team (and Mr. McMahon), decided that the billionaire was the right fit as his kayfabe father.

The storyline then saw Mr. McMahon try to show Hornswoggle "tough love," by forcing him to face much bigger and stronger opponents. This included Jamie Noble and The Great Khali. There was also a forgettable segment in which Vince viciously attacked Hornswoggle with a belt.

Long after the controversial storyline, Hornswoggle would see more successful runs in the WWE. The former Cruiserweight Champion became "the official mascot of D-Generation X," and supported Triple H and Shawn Michaels. He was also revealed to be the anonymous Raw general manager on the July 9, 2012 episode of the show.

Hornswoggle had a nice run in the WWE, and being loved by the fans for his underdog story and humorous character. The Vince McMahon father storyline may have been a regrettable decision by the creative team, but at least Hornswoggle was able to make a name for himself. He now works in the independent circuit, but perhaps a return to the WWE isn't out of he question.
