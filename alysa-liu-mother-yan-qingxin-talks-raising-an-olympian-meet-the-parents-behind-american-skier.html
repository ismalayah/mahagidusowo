Alysa Liu Mother Yan Qingxin Talks Raising An Olympian, Meet The Parents Behind American Skier

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Yan Qingxin is the mother of Alysa Liu, who nailed her debut performance as the youngest American on Team USA at the Winter Olympics 2022 in Beijing.

Alysa Liu, who at the age of 13 became the youngest American to win a national title, is also the youngest member of the Team USA contingent at Beijing 2022 in any sport.

The American figure skater had a good reason for cracking a wide grin on Olympic ice following her incredible debut performance, finishing eighth in the women’s short program with a score of 69.50.

Liu is in first place among her American counterparts, as reigning American champion Mariah Bell tripped on her first triple Lutz-triple toe combo.

Alysa Liu Mother: Yan Qingxin Talks Raising An Olympian

Yan Qingxin, the mother of Alysa Liu, talked about her excitement and challenges in raising an Olympian. However, she is not the biological mother of Alysa Liu.

According to The Focus News, Alysa and her siblings were produced through in vitro fertilization and delivered through surrogate mothers. Both of Liu’s donors were white women in the hopes that they would “benefit from a diversified gene pool.”

Yan Qingxin is also known as Mary and was married to Alysa’s father, Arthur Liu when she was born. Unfortunately, the couple is divorced, but the Liu children refer to her as mom and spend time with her regularly.

Arthur took his daughter to the ice when she was five years old, inspired by the successes of two-time Olympic figure skater Michelle Kwan. He’s known as the “full-fledged skating parent” and makes a checklist for his daughter before each practice.

Alysa presented her gold medal to her father when she won a competition in 2019.

Alysa Liu Age: How Old Is The Olympian?

Alysa Liu, the young talented figure skater, is 16 years of age. She was born on August 8, 2005.

Alysa is a naturally talented athlete, and she stands above 1.58 m. The young figure skater has beautiful black-colored eyes with brown hair, which fits her bold yet confident persona.

She often shares photographs of her travels, competitions, friends, and family with her 51.9k followers on Instagram.

Alysa became the youngest figure skater in U.S. history to land three triple axels in a single competition and win back-to-back titles in 2019 and 2020 at thirteen years old.

Those achievements have earned her the moniker “Simone Biles of figure skating,” among others. Alysa credits her success as a world-renowned skater to the assistance of her father, siblings, and family.

Yan Qingxin Family Details: How Many Daughters Does She Have?

Yan Qingxin, the mother of Alysa Liu, has two other daughters named Selina and Julia. She has two sons, Joshua and Justin.

The age of the triplets, Joshua, Justin, and Julia, is 11 years old. Selina is about 13 years of age. However, as mentioned above, Yan is not the biological mother of the Liu children.

Sports Illustrated stated that Arthur Liu had his children through in vitro fertilization, or IVF, using two distinct egg donors and two different surrogates.

According to the magazine, Mr. Liu desired a large family similar to his own in China and began looking for an egg donor. For almost three years, the family lived in a cramped one-bedroom apartment because of the high cost of the process.
