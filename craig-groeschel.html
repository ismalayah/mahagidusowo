Craig Groeschel

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Craig Groeschel is the founder and senior pastor of Life.Church, an American evangelical multi-site church with locations in 10 U.S. states. He is renowned for providing teaching, leadership, and guidance for the church. 

Besides being a well-known pastor, Craig is an American investor. Furthermore, he is husband to his wife, father to his kids, and grandfather to his grandchildren. Learn all about him with more insight on this page. Stay tuned till the end.

Craig Groeschel's Married Life: Wife Children

Craig Groeschel is a married man. He is happily married to his wife, Amy Groeschel. The duo tied the knot back in 1991, as noted by Everipedia.

Craig Groeschel's Career Details

As already mentioned, Craig Groeschel is the founder and senior pastor of Life.Church. He is invested in changing other people's lives, and his contribution is very notable in the religious world. 

Groeschel is one of the significant pastors in the world of Churches. Besides being a minister, he is also an excellent investor. Yes, he is, and although much is yet to be revealed on this topic, sources claim he is invested in some exceptional assets, which boost his net worth. 

Groeschel also hosts a podcast.
Photo Source: Listen Notes

Previously Groeschel served on the Board of Directors of Gulfport Energy Corporation. His 2016 compensation was a grant of 3,824 equity shares on June 10, 2016, with a value of $125,006.56 at the business's close that day. 

Later, on December 15, 2017, Groeschel was reported selling 7,059 shares of Gulfport Energy stock for $85,837. Subsequently, in November 2019, Gulfport Energy announced Groeschel's resignation from the board.

Craig Groeschel's Early life and Education Background

Craig Groeschel was born on December 2, 1967, in Houston, Texas. The 53-year-old minister grew up in southern Oklahoma. There he attended Ardmore High School. 

After completing high school, Groeschel went to Oklahoma City University on a tennis scholarship. He was a member of the Lambda Chi Alpha fraternity and received a bachelor's degree in Marketing.
