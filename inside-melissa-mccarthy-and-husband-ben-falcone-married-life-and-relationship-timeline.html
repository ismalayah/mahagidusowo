Inside Melissa McCarthy And Husband Ben Falcone Married Life And Relationship Timeline

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Relationship Timeline Between Melissa McCarthy And Husband Ben Falcone 

Melissa McCarthy and Ben Falcone first met in 1995 at a comedy writing class at The Groundlings School in L.A. Melissa told Ellen DeGeneres of their meeting, “we were friends from, like, day one.”

The duo has shared the screen plenty of time together, with Ben first making an appearance on Gilmore Girls, on which McCarthy played chef Sookis St. James in 2003. 

In 2013, the actress told More Magazine, “I got hit with the lucky stick with Ben”, to which her husband added, “We got hit with the same lucky stick. From the very first time we spoke, we were on the same page. The pair tied the knot in 2005 after dating for seven years. Since then, they have had two daughters together named Vivian in 2007 and Georgette in 2010. 

In 2012, Ben was there to support his wife when she was nominated for a Best Supporting Actress Oscar for her role in ‘The Bridesmaids.’ In the same year, Ben made a cameo in Melissa’s film “Identity Thief” as he was there in Atlanta, where the movie was filmed.

In 2015, Falcone was also there to support McCarthy when she was honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. The couple is not only a romantic partner but also a professional partner. Together they started On the Day Productions and have written and produced films like “Tammy,” “The Boss, and Thunder Force.” 

The pair were the definition of #CoupleGoals when they rocked up to the Vanity Fair Oscar Party in matching tracksuits. They have known each other for 28 years and seem to be enjoying every day so far.

How Much Age Gap Is There Between Melissa McCarthy And Her Husband

Melissa McCarthy was born on August 26, 1970, and her husband, Ben Falcone, was born on August 25, 1973. This makes exactly 3 years of age difference between them, Melissa being the older one. 

Melissa was named by the time as one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2016, and she has been featured multiple times in annual rankings of the highest-paid actresses in the world.

Family Ethnicity of Melissa McCarthy

Melissa McCarthy was raised on a farm in a large Catholic family. Her father is of Irish descent, and her mother is of English, German, and Irish ancestry.

Similarly, Ben Falcone is of Italian, English, German, and Irish descent.
