Charlie Axel Woods- Meet Son Of Tiger Woods and Elin Nordegren

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 23, 2024

Charlie Axel Woods is famous for being the single son or youngest child of a professional golfer, Tiger Woods and his ex-wife, Elin Nordegren. Tiger is known as one of the most notable athletes and one of the highest-earning golfer all the time. 

He was born on February 8, 2009, in the United States. He has a big sister named Sam Alexis Woods born on June 18, 2007. His grandparents are Barbro Holmberg, Kultida Woods, Earl Woods, and Thomas Nordegren. He is the cousin of a professional golfer, Cheyenne Woods.

Charlie Wins Junior Golf Tournaments

Yes, the 11 years old little boy Woods is following his dad's footsteps. Just like his dad, he continues to dominate junior golf tournaments. In August 2020, he secured a junior golf tournament by five strokes. He shot a 33 on 9 holes. 

Not only this but Axel Woods won another victory. He owns a South Florida tournament by three strokes. While his dad, Tiger tried to get into battle at The Northern Trust. Woods has spoken on GolfTV about Charlie's love for the game saying: 

“He’s starting to understand how to play. He’s asking me the right questions. I’ve kept it competitive with his par, so it’s been just an absolute blast to go out there and just, you know, be with him. It reminds me so much of me and my dad growing up.”
 

He claims that he loves teaching him the game of golf and watching him progress each day. He even told that he's jealous of his son’s smooth swing saying: "I wish I had his move, I analyze his swing". 

Parent's (Tiger and Elin) Failed Matrimony

Woods and Nordegren first met in the Open Championship of 2001. The duo was introduced by the Swedish golfer Jasper Parnevik.

After dating for three years, they married on October 5, 2004, at the Sandy Lane Resort in Barbados. Following the marriage, the husband-wife duo also shared two sweet kids, Charlie and Sam.

This well-going relationship took a turn in 2010. The couple filed for a divorce case by the beginning of 2010, shortly after the birth of their son. The case was finalized by August 23, 2010. Regarding the separation, Tiger said, "I was unfaithful. I had affairs. I cheated" during a press conference, 

Just before the divorce, a story was published by a Magazine which is later reported by The Guardian as Woods had an extramarital affair with New York City nightclub manager Rachel Uchitel. Soon after the incident, many other women added their names on the list.

Tiger and Elin's Life After Divorce

Following the divorce, Eldrick Tont Woods, aged 44, lived a single life until 2013. On March 2013, he announced his relationship with Olympic gold medal skier Lindsey Vonn. 

They were not much active in public and remained private most of the time. Similar to his previous relationship, this also ended in 2015. After splitting with Vonn, he started dating stylist Kristin Smith. 

After the breakup, Woods met his current girlfriend, Erica Herman in 2016. They are unitedly for more than three years. Meanwhile, Elin, 40, is enjoying her love life along with her boyfriend Jordan Cameron, the former NFL player. They welcomed to a son Arthur Nordegren Cameron in October 2019.

Swedish-born model and nanny Nordegren also dated a late American billionaire entrepreneur and philanthropist, Chris Cline. Unfortunately, he and his daughter Kameron Cline died in a helicopter crash in the Bahama on July 4, 2019. 

Staggering Net Worth in 2020

Meanwhile, Charlie Axel Woods has no source of income. However his dad, Tiger has a tremendous net worth of $800 million. He credits most of his income through his successful career in Golf. 

Further, Woods reportedly makes $50-60 million per year. As per the reports of 2008, he had earned more than a billion-dollar from his job. According to Golf Digest, he made $769,440,709 from 1996 to 2007. Also, he earned $63 million from his various endeavours between June 2019 and June 2020.

On the other hand, his mother, Elin Nordegren enjoys $200 million net worth. During the divorce settlement with Tiger in 2010, she further took Tiger's 20% net worth i.e $100 million.
