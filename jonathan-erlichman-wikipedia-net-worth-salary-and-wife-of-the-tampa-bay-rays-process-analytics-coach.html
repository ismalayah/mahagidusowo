Jonathan Erlichman is a Canadian professional baseball coach for the Tampa Bay Rays of Major League Baseball.

Jonathan Erlichman, nicknamed “J-Money” was born and raised in Yonge-Eglinton, Toronto, Ontario. He has a  cousin named Michael Cammalleri, who played in the National Hockey League for 15 seasons.

Jonathan Erlichman earned a bachelor’s degree in mathematics from Princeton University in 2012. He then worked as an intern for the Toronto Blue Jays and had obtained his first full-time job from the Rays in January 2013.

Jonathan Erlichman became the Toronto Blue Jays’ director of analytics in December 2016. In December 2018, the Rays named Erlichman to their coaching staff as MLB’s first process and analytics coach.
