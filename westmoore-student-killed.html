One Westmoore Student Killed While Three Injured In A Car Crash

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 6, 2024

On Thursday, a collision involving two automobiles transporting Westmoore High School students resulted in one fatality and three injuries.

According to Lt. Jeff Flaggert of the Oklahoma City Police Department, the collision happened around noon near the junction of SW 134 and Pennsylvania Avenue.

Three other pupils involved in the accident were sent to the hospital, while one kid passed away at the site, according to Flaggers.

Following a collision on Thursday afternoon in southwest Oklahoma City, at least one student was murdered, and numerous others were hurt, according to the authorities.

Westmoore High School students were indeed engaged in the collision, according to Moore Public Schools.

According to Oklahoma City Police, the collision happened near Southwest 134th Street and South Pennsylvania Avenue at about 12:45 p.m.

According to authorities, two pupils were hospitalized in critical condition. The collision also involved three automobiles.

The victim’s parents have been informed, but authorities have not yet disclosed the identity of the concerned victims.

The identity of the deceased student and the others who suffered injuries weren’t immediately known Thursday night. 

Two of the three hospitalized patients, according to Flaggert, are in critical condition, and the third patient is thought to be in serious condition.

A Moore Public Schools representative confirmed late on Thursday that police had informed district administrators about the incident.

“This evening, Moore Public Schools received confirmation from the Oklahoma City Police Department that several Westmoore High School students were engaged in the car collision at SW 134th and S Pennsylvania Avenue, which also claimed one student’s life. hospitals are currently caring for several students,” According to a statement issued by the district.

The statement added, “Our thoughts and prayers are with the families affected by this catastrophe and devastating loss.”

The following statement was made public by Moore Public Schools:

“This evening, the Oklahoma City Police Department informed Moore Public Schools that one student died due to the car collision at SW 134th and S. Penn Ave. involving several Westmoore High School students. Hospitalization is occurring for several students. The entire MPS family is mourning this sad loss. Tomorrow is the last day of classes, and the MPS Mental Health Team will be present to support students and staff at WHS. We condolences and prayers to the families impacted by this tragedy.”

According to the police, speed may have contributed to the collision.
