P-Valley Season 3: Everything You Need to Know

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 2, 2024

P-Valley is a hit drama series that follows the lives of the dancers and staff of the Pynk, a strip club in the Mississippi Delta.

The show, created by Katori Hall, is based on her play Pussy Valley and features a diverse cast of characters who deal with issues such as poverty, racism, sexuality, and identity.

P-Valley has received critical acclaim for its authentic portrayal of the Southern Black culture and the empowerment of women in the industry.

But when will the show return for its third season? Here is everything you need to know about P-Valley season 3.

When did P-Valley start?

P-Valley premiered on Starz on July 12, 2020, with eight episodes.

The show was an instant success, drawing in more than 8.1 million viewers across all platforms in its first week.

P-Valley also became the most-watched new series on Starz among African-American viewers.

The show was renewed for a second season on July 27, 2020, just two weeks after its debut.

The second season aired from June 6 to August 8, 2022, with ten episodes.

The show continued to receive positive reviews from critics and fans alike, earning a 100% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes.

P-Valley season 3 cast: Who will be in it?

P-Valley season 3 will likely bring back most of the main cast members from the previous seasons.

This includes:

Brandee Evans as Mercedes, the headliner of the Pynk who retired from dancing to pursue her dream of opening a dance studio.

Nicco Annan is Uncle Clifford, the owner and manager of the Pynk who is openly gay and non-binary.
Elarica Johnson as Autumn/Hailey, a newcomer to the Pynk who escaped from an abusive relationship and a money-laundering scheme.

Shannon Thornton as Keyshawn/Miss Mississippi, a young mother and dancer who rose to fame after shooting her abusive boyfriend.

J. Alphonse Nicholson as Lil’ Murda, an aspiring rapper who fell in love with Uncle Clifford and came out publicly at Ernestine’s party.

Parker Sawyers as Andre Watkins, a lawyer and developer who had an affair with Autumn and tried to buy out the land of the Pynk.

Harriett D. Foy is Patrice Woodbine, Mercedes’ mother and a fanatical preacher who stole her daughter’s money and disowned her.

Tyler Lepley as Diamond, the head of security at the Pynk who has a crush on Miss Mississippi.

Dan J. Johnson as Corbin Kyle, a biracial farmer and heir who partnered with Uncle Clifford to save the Pynk from foreclosure.

Morocco Omari as Mayor Ruffin, the corrupt mayor of Chucalissa who wanted to gentrify the area and get rid of the Pynk.

Loretta Devine as Ernestine Rivers, Uncle Clifford’s grandmother and former owner of the Pynk who passed away in season 2.

There is also a possibility that some new faces will join the cast in season 3, as well as some guest stars from previous seasons.

Is Season 3 of P-Valley coming out?

Yes, P-Valley season 3 is coming out, but not anytime soon.

The show was renewed for a third season in October 20225, but production was halted in May 2023 due to the ongoing Writers Guild of America strike.

The strike affected many shows that were filming or planning to film in Atlanta, where P-Valley is set and shot.

Showrunner Katori Hall said that she and her fellow writers would not resume work until a fair deal was reached.

As of now, there is no official word on when production will resume or when season 3 will premiere.

However, based on the previous seasons’ schedules, we can expect P-Valley season 3 to air sometime in late 2023 or early 2024.

Is there going to be a season 4 of P-Valley?

There is no confirmation yet on whether there will be a season 4 of P-Valley.

However, given the popularity and acclaim of the show, it is likely that Starz will renew it for another season.

Katori Hall has also hinted that she has more stories to tell about the characters and their world.

In an interview with Collider, she said: “It feels like the beginning, to be honest. I feel like we have so much more story to tell. These characters are so rich,  and I just want to continue to explore them and their journeys.”

Conclusion

P-Valley is a groundbreaking show that celebrates the resilience and beauty of the people who work at the Pynk, a strip club in the Mississippi Delta.

The show has been renewed for a third season, but fans will have to wait a while before they can see their favorite characters again.

P-Valley season 3 is expected to air in late 2023 or early 2024, depending on the resolution of the writers’ strike.

In the meantime, fans can rewatch the first two seasons on Starz or catch up on the show’s soundtrack, podcast, and social media.

P-Valley is a show that deserves all the love and support it can get.
